Scheme Highlights

Eligibility

Open for students in 10th, 12th, Polytechnic, and ITI courses with minimum 65% marks

Benefits

Free laptop with essential software and specifications for educational purposes

Easy Process

Simple online registration process with minimal documentation required

Eligibility Criteria

Residence

Must be a permanent resident of Uttar Pradesh

Academic Performance

Minimum 65% marks in 10th or 12th examinations

Course Eligibility

Open for 10th, 12th, Polytechnic, and ITI students

Documentation

Aadhar card, mark sheets, and residence proof required

Frequently Asked Questions

When will the UP Free Laptop Yojana 2025 start?

The exact start date is yet to be announced. Please keep checking the official website (www.up.gov.in) for updates.

What documents are required for registration?

You will need your Aadhar card, mark sheets (10th/12th), residence proof, and a valid mobile number for registration.

Is this scheme real or fake?

This is a genuine initiative by the Uttar Pradesh government to support students' education. You can verify this on the official government website (www.up.gov.in).

What are the benefits of this scheme?

The scheme provides free laptops to eligible students, enhancing their learning experience, providing access to online resources, and helping develop essential computer skills for future employment.
